# Week 2 — Challenges

1. **[Challenge 1 — Decode a public sample PCAP](challenge-01-decode-a-pcap.md)** — pick one capture from the Wireshark or Malware-Traffic-Analysis sample collections and produce a one-page analyst brief. (~3 h)

The sample PCAP collections used in this challenge are distributed by their authors for educational use. You are not capturing anyone's traffic — you are analysing a file someone else published for exactly this purpose. Read the chosen capture's host page for its terms before you commit your write-up.

Skip the challenge and you will know the lecture material. Do it and you will *feel* what an analyst's first hour with a PCAP looks like.
